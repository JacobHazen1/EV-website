Tackling Climate Change through Zero-Emission Vehicles

We propose that Canada’s strategy is to pursue the development of battery electric vehicles (BEV). BEV is chosen over hydrogen fuel cell vehicles due to its advanced stage of research development, growing market shares, and available charging infrastructure.

Find Out More

Three step plan with a fulfilment aim of 2032

Our 10-year plan will address the concerns mentioned below, as well as educating the general public on the benefits of EVs. This plan will supplement the Canadian government’s 2050 zero-emission plan.

The Plan

Incentives & Policies

Our priority is to lowering taxes and implement incentives for EV and home charging station purchases!

Charging station infrastructure

Starting in 2023, make it mandatory to install a charging outlet in every new home built!

Partnership with Canadian Businesses

Offering partnership initiatives for Canadian businesses, including financial grants!

Educate Canadians

Educate and improve public opinion on EV for the next generation of Canadian drivers!
